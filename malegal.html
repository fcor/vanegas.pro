The Challenge

Requests

Muñoz Aya is a law firma based in Bogotá, Colombia. They wanted to redesign their website with a special request. They wanted a "work with us" section to recive the aspirants applications with an aspirants CVs attached, because of they got just an email for this process and had to contact again the aspirants to request the Cvs, in other words they wanted to save time in this process.

Foundations
